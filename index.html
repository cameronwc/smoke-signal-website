Features

Everything You Need
Past the Last Cell Tower

Smoke Signal replaces the gear bag full of radios, paper maps, and hope. One app, one device, total group awareness.

Live Group Map

See every member of your group on a real-time map with GPS breadcrumb trails. Know exactly where everyone is, even deep in the backcountry.

TRK 04 · ACTIVE PEERS 3

Encrypted P2P Chat

Private by design. Messages travel device to device, never to a server or the cloud — and they're location-tagged so your group always has context.

Group Alert

Press the button to instantly ping your whole group with your exact location, so the people closest to you know right where you are.

GPX Import & Export

Import routes from GPX files or record your own with live elevation tracking. Export any route with full waypoint and elevation data.

Weather Overlay

Live weather conditions layered right on your map. Make smarter calls about when to push forward and when to find shelter.

Your Data, Your Device

All data lives on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no telemetry. Messages and locations move directly between devices — there's no server in the middle to leak, subpoena, or shut down.

Smoke Signal is not a satellite messenger and does not contact emergency services (911). Group Alert reaches only group members within radio range. For life-threatening emergencies, carry a satellite communicator or ensure cell coverage.

Setup

Up and Running in Minutes

No accounts, no subscriptions, no complicated setup. Power on your device and go.

  1. 01

    Get Your Device

    Your Tag or Pocket arrives pre-configured for your group. Nothing to source, nothing to flash — it works the moment it ships.

    Pair Over Bluetooth

    Open the app, turn on your device, and they connect automatically. No accounts to create, no cloud to configure.

    Add Your Group
