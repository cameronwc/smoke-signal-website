Features

Everything You Need
Past the Last Cell Tower

Smoke Signal replaces the gear bag full of radios, paper maps, and hope. One app, one tracker, total group awareness.

📍

Live Group Map

See every member of your group on a real-time map with GPS breadcrumb trails. Know exactly where everyone is, even deep in the backcountry.

💬

Offline Messaging

Send and receive text messages between devices without any internet connection. Messages are location-tagged so your group always has context.

🚨

Emergency SOS

Triple-press the tracker button to instantly broadcast an emergency alert with your exact GPS coordinates to every connected group member.

🗺️

Route Planning & Recording

Plan your route with waypoints before you leave. Auto-record your actual path as you go. Export to GPX or KML to share with others.

🌦️

Weather Overlay

Live weather conditions layered right on your map. Make smarter decisions about when to push forward and when to find shelter.

🔋

Tracker Battery Monitoring

Always know your tracker's battery level right from the app. No surprises when you're 20 miles from the trailhead.

How It Works

Up and Running in Minutes

No accounts, no subscriptions, no complicated setup. Pair your tracker and go.

Grab a Tracker

Pick up a SenseCAP T1000-E — a rugged, pocket-sized GPS tracker with Bluetooth and long battery life built for the outdoors.

Pair with Smoke Signal

Open the app, turn on your tracker, and they connect automatically over Bluetooth. No accounts to create, no cloud to configure.

Add Your Group

Other Smoke Signal users nearby are discovered automatically. Everyone in your group appears on the shared map in real time.

Go Explore

Hit the trail. Your group stays connected with live locations, offline chat, and emergency SOS — no cell towers required.
