What Is Smoke Signal?

Your Whole Group, Connected Off the Grid

Smoke Signal is an off-grid group-communication system — a small mesh-radio device paired with an iPhone app — that lets your group text each other and see everyone on a live map with no cell service, no internet, and no monthly fee.

It's built for outdoor groups who aren't technical — hiking, hunting, backcountry skiing, paddling, overlanding, and festivals. Each person carries a device, and the devices relay for one another, so messages and locations reach the whole group even when you're spread out. It's a one-time purchase: no subscription and no per-message fees.

Smoke Signal is not a satellite messenger and does not contact emergency services (911). Its Group Alert feature reaches only group members within radio range. For life-threatening emergencies, carry a satellite communicator or ensure cell coverage.

How It Works

Up and Running in Minutes

No accounts, no subscriptions, no complicated setup. Power on your device and go.

  1. 01 /

    Get Your Smoke Signal Device

    Your Tag or Pocket arrives pre-configured for your group. Nothing to source, nothing to set up — it works the moment it ships.

  2. 02 /

    Pair with Smoke Signal

    Open the app, turn on your device, and they connect automatically over Bluetooth. No accounts to create, no cloud to configure.

  3. 03 /
